## 🇺🇸 English

### 1. Information We Collect

Lockin Focus collects and stores only the following:
- **Focus score and activity history**: daily scores, focus session durations, earned badges. All stored on this device only and never transmitted to an external server.
- **Nickname (optional)**: a string you choose. Only stored in Apple iCloud Public Database if you opt into the leaderboard, where it becomes visible to other users.
- **Leaderboard identifier**: a device UUID. Not linked to your Apple ID, name, or email.

We do NOT collect:
- Personal identifiers (name, email, phone)
- Location
- Contacts, photos, media
- Advertising identifier (IDFA)
- Usage history of other apps on your device (we use Screen Time API but receive only aggregate hints, never stored)

### 2. Where Data Lives

- **Local data** (scores, badges, settings, passcode): on-device only. Removed when you uninstall. Passcode is stored encrypted in iOS Keychain.
- **Leaderboard data** (nickname, scores): Apple CloudKit Public Database. Managed by Apple's infrastructure; we operate no servers.
- **iCloud Key-Value Store**: for nickname sync across your Apple ID devices. Shared only within your Apple account.

### 3. Data Deletion

- **Local data**: automatically removed when you delete the app.
- **Leaderboard record**: delete via the in-app admin panel ("Delete my ranking record") or by emailing us. Deletion request: **imurmkj@naver.com**

### 4. Third Parties

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. We do not communicate with any external server except Apple's CloudKit.

### 5. Minors

This app is designed for adults managing their own focus. It is not a parental-control tool and is not directed at children under 13.

### 6. Changes

If this policy changes we will notify you in-app and update the Last updated date above.

### 7. Contact

Privacy inquiries: **imurmkj@naver.com**

- **लीडरबोर्ड पहचानकर्ता**: डिवाइस-स्तर UUID। आपकी Apple ID, नाम, या ईमेल से जुड़ा नहीं।
